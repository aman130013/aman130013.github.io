Dr. Amandeep Dhaliwal is a distinguished academic leader and Research Director at the School of Leadership & Management, Manav Rachna International Institute of Research & Studies, Faridabad. With a doctoral degree from BIMTECH–Mohan Lal Sukhadia University, her scholarly trajectory is anchored in the intersection of marketing, digital entrepreneurship, and analytics.

Over a career spanning more than sixteen years in higher education — complemented by five years of corporate experience — she has developed a reputation as a rigorous scholar and mentor. Her research has been published in Scopus Q1 journals including Food and Humanity and Language & Communication, and she has contributed to Springer, Taylor & Francis, Wiley, and IGI Global volumes.

She has provided strategic leadership on NAAC, NBA, and UGC accreditation, guided Outcome-Based Education (OBE) implementation, and championed research capacity building across the institution. Her work bridges the theoretical and the applied, with an enduring commitment to student development and academic quality.
